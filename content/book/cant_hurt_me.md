My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/8144849264">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
David Goggins has a way to inspire people to be better and push themselves, and this book is all about it!<br /><br />​I say this even though the book is weird at points - especially when the army instructors speak. I respect that the writer didn't leave it out and tried to paint everything in as real colors as possible, though it's not for everyone.<br /><br />​As a bonus, it gave me the drive to run, go to the gym, and push myself whenever I read it. That's why I'm giving it 5/5!
