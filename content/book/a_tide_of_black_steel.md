<br /><br />
For me it's a did-not-finish book. <br />The writing is very verbose, so the book does not flow very well -> the story is slow to move and nothing really happens. <br />It includes lots of special world building terms (like people's professions or geographic terms) that I had to look up to understand. <br />The world is not really explained well so I also had to look up how that world works, since it has ties to Ryan's other books.<br />And the characters are one dimensional and pretty bland so far. Not really interesting. <br />I read until the end of part 1, so all of this might have changed, but be warned that this is a very slow book at the beginning - to get used to it and all.
