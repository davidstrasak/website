My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/7436564368">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
Wow, this book was amazing. It gets a bit dark at times, but it's really well written and pretty funny.<br /><br />It's best strength is that it has tons of set ups for different jokes or plot points, but also tons of punchlines. I can totally see why the series is as big as it. <br /><br />If every book has new totally wild ideas and resolves the older wild ideas, it's gonna be a lot of fun and lots of good reading.<br /><br />This was my first literary RPG book and the quirks of this genre are also pretty cool. The fact that the characters use the mechanics of the game to their advantage makes for some crazy story moments. I will check this genre out further :D
